static

Searches scf.foreach_thread ops nested under target and maps each such op to GPU threads.

Mapping is one-to-one and the induction variables of scf.foreach_thread are rewritten to gpu.thread_id according to the thread_dim_apping attribute. Sibling scf.foreach_thread are supported in which case, the union of the number of threads is computed and may result in predication. Dynamic, scf.foreach_thread trip counts are currently not supported. Dynamic block dim sizes are currently not supported.
